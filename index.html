Features

Everything a serious eBay seller actually needs.

🧠
AI Listing Generation
Generates keyword-rich titles up to 80 chars, professional descriptions with specs and condition, and realistic price suggestions in under 3 seconds.
🔄
Regenerate Any Field Popular
Don't like the title? Click regenerate next to it. Every output field has its own regenerate button — you get a completely fresh AI response for just that field.
🗂️
Full eBay US Category Tree
Every eBay US category available as a searchable multi-level dropdown. Select Electronics → Audio → Portable Players and ListAI instantly shows the exact item specifics eBay requires.
🖼️
Product Image Upload Pro
Upload up to 12 product photos per listing. Images attach directly to your eBay listing via the API. Drag to reorder. On Pro, AI reads your photos to auto-extract product details.
📊
CSV Bulk Upload
Upload a spreadsheet with 1 to 100 products at once. Any column headers work — Title, SKU, Condition, Notes — ListAI maps them automatically. All listings generated in parallel.
📡
Live eBay Status Tracking
Dashboard shows real-time status pulled directly from eBay: Active, Watching count, Sold, Ended, Failed. After every batch publish you get a full success/failure report with eBay item IDs.
